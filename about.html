Our Story

Built on Trust,
Driven by Quality

Shree Ganesh Furnishing was founded in 2017 with a simple but powerful vision: to bring world-class furnishing solutions to every home and business in Gujarat at transparent, affordable prices.

Starting from Mehsana, we have grown to serve thousands of happy customers across all of Gujarat — from individual residences to large commercial complexes, hospitals, schools, and industrial facilities.

What makes us different is our commitment to customization without compromise. Whether you bring us a photo from Google, Pinterest, Instagram, or any reference — we will craft and install exactly that design, to your exact measurements, in your exact space.

Every installation comes backed by our 7 to 10 year warranty, because we stand behind the quality of our work and the materials we use.

Premium Materials

We Only Use
The Best

We exclusively work with premium, industry-certified brands that have proven their quality and durability over decades.

Primary Material Brand

SRP Brand

SRP (Standard Reinforced Products) is one of India's most trusted names in net and curtain materials. Known for exceptional tensile strength, UV resistance, and long service life — the material of choice for premium installations.

High tensile strength — doesn't sag or stretch over time
UV-resistant coating for outdoor and semi-outdoor use
Rust-proof frame components — lasts 7-10 years
Available in multiple mesh sizes and colors
Certified safe for children and pets
Premium Curtain Brand

VF Brand

VF (Verified Fabric) brand curtains represent the pinnacle of commercial-grade curtain materials. From heavy-duty industrial PVC strips to elegant residential fabrics — VF ensures beauty and durability in every fiber.

Commercial-grade PVC for industrial curtains
Premium fabric weaves for residential elegance
Thermal and acoustic insulation properties
Easy-clean and stain-resistant surfaces
Wide range of colors, patterns, and textures
